Research Summary

With a semester of heavy primary research to understand the literature and look for gaps to design an experimental method that can add value to the existing knowledge in psychology, I had the main objective of collecting data from the community student body on campus and finding any significant differences to my research question. After collecting and analyzing the data, I found there is a positive correlation between resilience and EI for the first hypothesis. However, the data did not support the second and third hypotheses. Overall, I learned to be curious and ask questions to problem-solve because a step back is another chance to move forward in the research field.
